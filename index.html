About Agape counseling

Agape Counseling is a ministry to the world. Through our counseling center, people can come 24/7 to get help.

Our mission is to help counsel people through a biblical lens. Many people go to psychotherapy, but we believe the Bible is sufficient for all our needs.

Our theme verse is 2 Timothy 3:16-17 which says "All Scripture is breathed out by God and profitable for teaching, for reproof, for correction, and for training in righteousness, that the man of God may be complete, equipped for every good work."

Contact us
